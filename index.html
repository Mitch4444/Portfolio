About me

Hi, I'm Mitchell. I'm a Web Developer based in Arizona with a hunger for learning new things. Currently, I am a student at the Full Stack Flex program at Thinkful. Figuring out how to overcome obstacles in coding is satisfying for me because I am a natural problem solver and I like sharing my findings with co-workers. I am more comfortable and experienced with the front-end of web development, however, I am excited to learn more about the back-end and get more hands-on experience to better understand it. I have the determination to see things through to their final product and I am always willing to learn more.

On an average day, I enjoy sharpening my coding skills, playing video games, being outdoors, playing sports, and being with my fiance and puppy.
